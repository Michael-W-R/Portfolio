# Michael W. R — Portfolio

This portfolio highlights the digital products, workflows, and modernization tools I’ve designed and built across my Army roles and independent projects. My work focuses on reducing friction for Soldiers, improving readiness, and creating intuitive, scalable solutions that support the entire force.

Because many of my Army projects require CAC access, this portfolio provides non-sensitive case studies, UI/UX workflows, screenshots, and product summaries that demonstrate my design thinking, technical capability, and approach to solving real operational problems.

## Flagship Projects

- **[PCS Scout](https://pcsscout.com)** — a live, public web app I designed and
  built end-to-end that helps Soldiers and Families find housing during PCS
  moves: official DoD BAH, VA-loan affordability, rush-hour commute modeling,
  dual-workplace "fair commute" zones, and school data. *(Live site;
  [source](https://github.com/Michael-W-R/pcs-housing-finder).)*
- **[Senior Rater Profile Manager](Army-Projects/Senior-Rater-Profile-Manager.md)**
  — a Power BI rule engine encoding AR 623-3 evaluation-profile logic with a
  "would process as" misfire check.
- **[Strength Management Tool](Army-Projects/Strength-Management-Tool.md)** — a
  snapshot star-schema Power BI suite for authorized-vs-assigned strength and
  forward force projection.
