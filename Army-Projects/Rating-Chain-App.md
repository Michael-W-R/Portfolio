# Rating Chain App — Case Study

## Overview
The Rating Chain App is a Soldier-facing tool designed to simplify the creation, validation, and management of rating chains during standard operations and periods of organizational transition. I led the product design and workflow development, ensuring the tool aligned with Army policy while remaining intuitive for commanders, staff sections, and HR professionals.

## Problem
Units frequently struggle with rating chain accuracy during reorganizations, deployments, and transitions between components. Manual processes lead to errors, delays, and inconsistent policy interpretation. Soldiers often do not know who their rating officials are, and commanders lack visibility into chain completeness.

## Solution
The Rating Chain App provides:
- A guided workflow for building compliant rating chains  
- Automated validation against AR 623-3 and DA PAM 623-3  
- Clear, Soldier-friendly UI for viewing rating officials  
- Integration with unit rosters and organizational structures  
- Tools for commanders to review, approve, and publish chains  

## My Role
I designed the user experience, mapped workflows, and translated policy requirements into functional logic. I conducted user discovery with commanders and HR professionals, built prototypes, and collaborated with developers to refine the interface and logic.

## Impact
- Reduced rating chain errors  
- Improved commander visibility  
- Faster onboarding during organizational transitions  
- More consistent policy compliance across units
