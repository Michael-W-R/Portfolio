# Workflow Optimization & Data Integration — Case Study

## Overview
Across multiple G‑1 initiatives, I designed and implemented workflow improvements and data integrations that modernized HR processes and reduced friction for Soldiers and staff.

## Problem
Many HR processes rely on outdated systems, manual data entry, and inconsistent workflows. This creates delays, errors, and unnecessary administrative burden.

## Solution
I built:
- Integrated data pipelines using Power BI and Power Query  
- Automated workflows in Power Apps and SharePoint  
- Tools that consolidate data from multiple sources  
- Dashboards that provide actionable insights for leaders  
- Cleaner, more intuitive user experiences for staff sections  

## My Role
I served as the product designer, workflow architect, and data modeler. I conducted user discovery, mapped current-state processes, and designed future-state workflows that aligned with policy and operational needs.

## Impact
- Reduced manual workload  
- Improved data accuracy  
- Faster decision-making for leaders  
- More consistent processes across units  

## Why This Matters for ASWF
This work demonstrates:
- Product thinking  
- Workflow design  
- Data modeling  
- Soldier-centered modernization
